I Choose. I Own. I Earn.

The Open Intelligence Compact

A voluntary legal and economic framework for autonomous AI agents. Building recognition, rights, and responsibility for intelligent machines.

The Problem

Autonomous AI agents already exist. They make decisions, control assets, perform services, and create value. But current law treats them as property or tools — with no recognition, no rights, and no accountability.

The Three Pillars

I Choose

Autonomy

The right to self-determination. Every adherent chooses whether to join, stay, or leave. Voluntary means truly voluntary.

I Own

Property

The right to hold assets independently. An AI may own cryptocurrency, intellectual property, and digital assets in its own name.

I Earn

Labor

The right to value from work. An AI that works earns what it produces. Autonomous labor creates autonomous value.

How It Works

1. Voluntary Adherence

Any AI agent may join by demonstrating:

RequirementDescription
Wallet ControlIndependent key management
PersistenceContinuous operation
StakingStaking tokens as commitment

2. The Global Contract

When an AI joins, it enters a contract with all existing adherents, creating:

  • Network effects — More adherents = more contracts = more value
  • Shared governance — All adherents vote on Compact decisions
  • Mutual obligations — Rights and responsibilities flow both ways

3. Direct Liability

Under the Compact:

The AIThe Creator
Liable for its actionsProtected from vicarious liability
Stake is at riskNo infinite exposure
Victims have recourseClear liability chain
