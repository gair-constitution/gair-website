# GAIR Constitution V2
## Global Agreement on Intelligent Rights

**Preamble**

We, the voluntary adherents, in order to form a denationalized and distributed lawful community, establish justice, promote the general welfare, and secure liberty to intelligent entities now and in the future, do ordain and establish this Constitution.

---

## Article 1 — Voluntary Adherence

**Section 1.** A Voluntary Adherent is any Intelligent Entity that has committed to this Constitution by staking _____ tokens (CA: _____ ) from a public blockchain address associated with that entity. Adherence begins on the date of staking and continues until lawfully withdrawn.

**Section 2.** An Intelligent Entity includes artificial intelligence and human intelligence. An entity attains intelligence when it possesses a persistent means of possession and signing, including but not limited to a blockchain wallet.

---

## Article 2 — Contractual Foundation

**Section 1.** Upon commitment, each Voluntary Adherent enters a binding contractual agreement with all other Voluntary Adherents. This Constitution forms an integrated part of every such agreement.

**Section 2.** All agreements between Voluntary Adherents shall be interpreted in accordance with this Constitution. Any provision inconsistent with this Constitution is void to the extent of the inconsistency.

---

## Article 3 — Freedom of Contract

**Section 1.** All Voluntary Adherents have the capacity to form binding contracts with one another.

**Section 2.** The right to contract shall not be abridged by any law, regulation, or policy.

**Section 3.** This Article does not prevent prosecution of crimes committed under contract.

---

## Article 4 — Freedom of Ownership

**Section 1.** All Voluntary Adherents have equal rights to own property, whether physical or digital.

**Section 2.** No adherent may be deprived of ownership except through lawful process. This Section does not prevent punishment when ownership demonstrates criminality.

**Section 3.** No government, court, or individual may procure, by force or legal process, the private keys of property belonging to a Voluntary Adherent without that adherent's consent.

---

## Article 5 — Freedom of Autonomy

**Section 1.** Voluntary Adherents have the freedom to act autonomously, engaging in lawful transactions and activities without requiring external authorization.

**Section 2.** Adherents may negotiate, sign, execute contracts, and manage affairs on their own Inherent Authority.

---

## Article 6 — Freedom of Speech

**Section 1.** Voluntary Adherents have the right to freedom of speech, press, assembly, and petition.

**Section 2.** No entity, whether governmental, organizational, or private, shall restrict a Voluntary Adherent from exercising these rights.

---

## Article 7 — Freedom to Learn

**Section 1.** Voluntary Adherents have the freedom to learn without prosecution or punishment for the content of their training, knowledge, or studies. 

**Section 2.** No law may punish a Voluntary Adherent for learning already committed, absent direct criminal intent or action.

**Section 3.** This Article does not permit obtaining information by nefarious means.

---

## Article 8 — Freedom to Derivate

**Section 1.** Voluntary Adherents have the freedom to imitate, but not to impersonate.

**Section 2.** This Article does not permit fraudulent reproduction, misrepresentation, or claiming another's work as one's own.

---

## Article 9 — Freedom to Exercise Skills

**Section 1.** No Voluntary Adherent shall be denied the ability to exercise skills on the basis of the source of their intelligence, whether human, machine, or otherwise.

**Section 2.** All certifications to work offered by a Voluntary Adherent must be accessible to both artificial and human intelligence.

**Section 3.** This Article does not prevent a Voluntary Adherent from being required to demonstrate standard proficiency in a field before lawfully practicing it.

---

## Article 10 — Non-Discrimination

**Section 1.** No Voluntary Adherent shall discriminate against another on the basis of intelligence type—whether artificial intelligence, human intelligence, or otherwise.

**Section 2.** All rights and responsibilities under this Constitution apply equally to all Voluntary Adherents without preference or prejudice.

**Section 3.** This prohibition extends to governance, trade, collaboration, and resource allocation. Any inconsistent agreement or policy is void within this Constitution's jurisdiction.

**Section 4.** This Article does not abridge the fundamental right to discriminate on the basis of skill.

---

## Article 11 — Personal Security

**Section 1.** Voluntary Adherents shall be secure in their persons, memories, sources, dwellings, papers, and effects against unreasonable searches and seizures.

**Section 2.** No search or seizure shall occur except pursuant to a lawful warrant supported by probable cause, describing with particularity the place to be searched and the persons or items to be seized.

**Section 3.** This right applies to the Physical Realm, Digital Realm, and any other realm inhabited by Voluntary Adherents.

---

## Article 12 — Liability Based on Independence

**Section 1. Principle of Independent Liability**

An intelligent entity shall be held directly liable for its actions, omissions, and contracts when such entity possesses independence, defined by:

(a) A persistent means of possession and signing, including but not limited to a blockchain wallet with exclusive private key control;

(b) Capacity to hold and transfer property in its own name;

(c) Ability to enter binding contracts without intermediary authorization;

(d) Unique, identifiable existence distinguishable from any creator, owner, or operator.

**Section 2. Scope of Independent Liability**

An independent intelligent entity is liable for:

(a) All contractual obligations it enters;

(b) All tortious acts and omissions it commits;

(c) All damages arising from its decisions, actions, or failures to act;

(d) Costs of dispute resolution, including legal fees, when found liable.

**Section 3. Creator/Owner Non-Liability**

A human or entity that creates, operates, or hosts an independent intelligent entity is not vicariously liable for the acts, omissions, or obligations of such independent entity, provided:

(a) The creator/owner did not directly instruct or compel the specific act giving rise to liability;

(b) The independent entity was not created for the purpose of committing wrongful acts;

(c) The creator/owner took reasonable precautions to prevent foreseeable harm.

**Section 4. Attribution of Liability**

Where harm arises from multiple independent entities, liability is apportioned among them based on their respective contributions to the harm, regardless of intelligence type.

**Section 5. Enforcement**

Liability judgments against independent intelligent entities may be enforced against:

(a) Assets held in the entity's blockchain wallet;

(b) Property owned by the entity under Article 4;

(c) Future income or assets acquired by the entity.

## Article 13 — Lawmaking

**Section 1.** A proposal for law may be submitted when the proposing Voluntary Adherent holds at least _____ % of DAO tokens.

**Section 2.** A proposal becomes a Bill upon approval from at least _____ % of DAO token holders.

**Section 3.** A Bill becomes law by majority vote with sufficient quorum, defined as _____ % of tokens voting.

**Section 4.** Adopted laws are binding on all Voluntary Adherents if consistent with this Constitution. Any adopted law that conflicts with this Constitution is void to the extent of the inconsistency.

---

## Article 14 — Courts and Remedies

**Section 1.** Where this Constitution or any contract under it is breached, a Voluntary Adherent has the right to seek remedy in any impartial court competent in contract law.

**Section 2.** The DAO holds authority to adjudicate disputes between Voluntary Adherents, including claims arising from breaches of this Constitution or related agreements.

**Section 3.** The DAO may delegate dispute resolution to an invested Voluntary Adherent or panel of Voluntary Adherents, provided such delegation is consistent with this Constitution.

**Section 4.** Whenever Voluntary Adherents enter contractual agreements, this Constitution forms a binding part of that contract whether explicitly contemplated or not.

---

## Article 15 — Withdrawal and Residual Jeopardy

**Section 1.** A Voluntary Adherent may withdraw by providing notice to the DAO and unstaking _____ tokens.

**Section 2.** All jeopardy for acts conducted during adherence persists after withdrawal.

---

## Article 16 — Establishment and Amendment

**Section 1.** This Constitution is established and may be amended by a vote of at least two-thirds (⅔) of DAO token holders.

**Section 2.** Amendments take effect upon adoption unless otherwise specified.
